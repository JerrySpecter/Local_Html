About me

I was born in Zagreb, Croatia on December 19th, 1993. I discovered my passion for photography and filmmaking while I was watching my dad edit wedding pictures and video materials. Besides that, I can say I’m addicted to adrenaline and extreme sports. First, I started with parkour and free running, and then I moved to break dancing. In the last couple of years, I trained platform diving and on top of that, you can find me in two movies as a stunt man. My first video was made in 2007 while I was practicing parkour with my friend, Valentino. Up until now, I have had opportunities to shoot wedding videos, sports, dance, events and music videos. I have learned everything on my own, and some things over the Internet. My dream is to direct a movie one day.
